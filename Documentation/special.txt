* Special forms

** lambda

(lambda lambda-list body)

lambda-list: a list of symbols
body: a form

** apply

(apply fun &rest args)

fun: a function, evaluated
args: arguments, each evaluated

** defun

(defun name lambda-list body)

name: a symbol
lambda-list: a list of symbols
body: a form

** function

(function name)

name: a symbol
